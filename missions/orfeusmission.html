Mission

The orefeus-spas I mission flew in September of 1993 and acquired astronomical data. It performed ultraviolet spectroscopy. Orfeus spas ii launched in November of 1996 with an objective to study celestial ultraviolet light sources.

Mythology

Orpheus was a man who traveled to the underworld in order to save the life of his beloved. Hades told him that he could rescue her, but while they were walking, he was not allowed to look back at her. If he did, Hades would take her back. There are a few versions of this myth, but a well-known one is that he heard her fall, so he looked back to help her. Hades ended up taking her back forever. Orpheus then left the underworld, one of the only mortals to ever do so. The Orfeus was named after him because it was designed to explore the deep "hadal" zone, referencing the underworld.
