Mission

Janus was a spacecraft with an objective of studying different orbits of two different asteroid systems. The mission however was cancelled in July of 2023, placing the spacecraft into long-term storage. It planned to image asteroids in visible and infrared light. The plan was approved in 2020, but was cancelled due to Psyche's launch date being moved.

Mythology

Janus was a roman god of beginnings and endings. He had two faces. The spacecraft was named after him for sending two identical spacecrafts to study two distinct asteroid systems. Janus was a primordial diety, serving for both war and peace. Janus has no greek equivalent, due to existing in the Roman religion before they adopted the Greek pantheon. He could be associated with Hermes or Hecate, however. No god shares his two-faced nature.
