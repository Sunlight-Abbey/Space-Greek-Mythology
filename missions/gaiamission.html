Mission

Gaia's mission was launched in December of 2013. It's goal was to create the largest 3D map of the Milky Way. It would achieve this by surveying about 1% of the galaxy's 100 billion stars. These stars would be observed an average of 70 times over five years.

Mythology

In Greek mythology, Gaia was the personification of the Earth. She could also be known as Mother Earth. The reason it was named after her is to signify its role in mapping our galaxy from an Earth-based perspective. Its mission was to reflect the galaxy from Earth's point of view. Gaia created everything, including Greek monsters to torment Zeus for locking the titans in Tartarus.
