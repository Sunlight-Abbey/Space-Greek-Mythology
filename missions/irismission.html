Mission

Iris launched in June of 2013. Its goal is to observe solar material, how it moves, gathering energy, and heating up as it travels through the sun's lower atmosphere. There is not much info known at the time due to Stanford University flooding in November 2024, however the Iris is okay.

Mythology

<

Iris was the greek goddess of the rainbow and Hera's messenger. NASA named their spacecraft after her to highlight its mission of studying the sun's interface region, the name chosen to reflect the "rainbow" of spectral data it gathers. She linked heaven and earth, traveling on the rainbow to deliver divine messages. She carries messages to mortals from Zeus and Hera, especially shown during the Illiad.
