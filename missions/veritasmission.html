Mission Name

Mission

Veritas plans to launch sometime after 2031. Its objective is to study Venus. It will be the first spacecraft to explore Venus since the 1990's. Veritas will discover the secrets of it, gathering data to reveal how paths of Venus and Earth diverged.

Mythology

Veritas is the roman goddess of truth and virtue. The NASA mission is named after her because of its objective to obtain data from Venus and discover the secrets of it, like how the goddess is the goddess of truth. She was considered a daughter of either Saturn or Jupiter.
