Mission Name

Mission

Hermes is an instrument package, aimed to launch after 2027. Its goal is to study the causes of space-weather variability. It will support Artemis lunar operations. It will study solar and cosmic radiation to help science understand how to keep people safe during deep space travels. It will fly outside the lunar space station.

Mythology

Hermes was the greek god of speed and the messenger of the gods. NASA named their mission after him to signify their goal of rapidly transmitting data and messages from deep space, the HERMES serving as NASA's own messenger. He was the son of Zeus and Maia, another spacecraft. While being their messenger, he was also known as a trickster god.
