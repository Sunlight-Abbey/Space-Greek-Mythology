Mission

Libera is planned to launch in September of 2027 in order to measure solar radiation. It will record how much energy leaves our planet's atmosphere on a day-by-day basis. This impacts the foundational science needed to explore our universe, especially places like the Moon and Mars.

Mythology

Libera is the roman goddess of freedom and fertility. The spacecraft is named after it because the planets energy flows free, libera monitoring the balances and cycles. Libera is the roman equivalent of Ariadne, the wife of Dionysus, the god of parties and wine.
