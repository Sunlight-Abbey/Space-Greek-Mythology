Mission

Terra launched in December of 1999, aiming to gain an understanding of the interactions between Earth's land, atmosphere, oceans, and biology. It provides insight into how the Earth system is changing, revealing humanity's impact on Earth, and providing important data about natural disasters.

Mythology

In Roman mythology, Terra was the personification of the Earth. She could also be known as Mother Earth. The reason it was named after her is to signify its role in understanding of Earth's land. Its mission was to reflect the galaxy from Earth's point of view. Gaia created everything, including Greek monsters to torment Zeus for locking the titans in Tartarus.
