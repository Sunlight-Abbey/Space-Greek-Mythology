Mission

The Venus Express launched in November of 2005, studying the atmosphere of venus and its environment. It was expected to last no more than 500 days, but the mission was extended to a total of 2,500 days. It was the first European spacecraft to orbit Venus. The mission ended in December of 2014.

Mythology

The Venus Express was named that after its mission to explore Venus. In Roman mythology, Venus is the goddess of beauty and love, as well as the lover of Mars. She was an equivalent to the greek Aphrodite and was considered the mother of Rome. Julius Caesar claimed to be her descendant. They say she emerged in adult form from sea foam.
