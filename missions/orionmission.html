Mission Name

Mission

Orion is a spacecraft that serves as an exploration vehicle for the Artemis II mission. It is carrying 4 passengers, and the mission duration is up to 21 days. It is one of NASA's newest spacecrafts. As of April 2026, it is an active mission currently in Space.

Mythology

Orion Spacecraft was named after the Orion constellation, a constellation that explorers use to guide their direction due to how it is visible worldwide. The name was selected for this to represent the spirit of exploration and its connection to the stars. In Mythology, Orion was a huntsman who Zeus and Artemis placed among the stars. In the Odyssey, Odysseus sees him hunting in the underworld.
