Atlas Mission

Mission

The Atlas Missiles were created to show technological strength and act as a deterrent during the cold war. There is also another ATLAS, the Asteroid Terrestrial-impact Last Alert System. It was created to watch and detect asteroids close to Earth, especially dangerous ones.

Mythology

In Greek Mythology, Atlas was a titan. During the war between the titans and the olympians, Atlas sided with the titans. After the olympians won the war, Atlas was punished and sent to hold up the sky on his shoulders for all of eternity. The Atlas missiles were named after Atlas because they carried rockets into the sky. The telescope was named after Atlas because like him, they served to protect and watch the sky.
