Mission

Muse plans to launch in 2027. Its objective is to study the physics of the solar atmosphere, and capture the clearest images of the corona. That is the outermost region of the sun, whose eruptions are the foundation of space weather. By observing the sun's extreme ultraviolet radiation and capturing high resolution images, this can become possible.

Mythology

The Muses were the personifications of knowledge, art, music, comedy, astronomy, and more. The MUSE was named after this because of its mission to take important photos of the sun, personifying this into a piece of art. There were 9 muses, all daughters of Zeus. Urania, the muse of astronomy, shares an etemology with Uranus, one of the planets in Earth's solar system. They were followers of Apollo, another mission.
