Mission

Europa Clipper was created to study one of Jupiter's moons, Europa. It launched October 2024 and plans to reach Jupiter in April of 2030, traveling about 1.8 billion miles. Once it arrives, it will orbit Jupiter and conduct flybys of Europa.

Mythology

In greek Mythology, Europa was a consort of Zeus, the king of the gods. In Roman mythology, Zeus is connected to Jupiter, otherwise known as the planet that the Europa moon belongs to. Essentially, its a moon named after one of Jupiter's affair partners. In the mythology, Zeus took Europa to his birthplace to hide from his wife. She then became the queen of his birthplace, known as Crete.
