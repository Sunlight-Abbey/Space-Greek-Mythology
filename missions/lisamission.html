Mission

Lisa plans to launch in the mid 2030's. It plans on detecting gravitational waves, ripples in fabric of space-time produced by systems and merging black holes. Gravitational waves provide different information about their sources than light does. It will push our understanding of cosmic physics.

Mythology

Lisa was named after the West African god, Mawu-Lisa. He represented the sun and male strength. Lisa is the male side, while Mawu is the female side. She represents wisdom and the moon. Together they are balanced. They emerge as merged twins.
