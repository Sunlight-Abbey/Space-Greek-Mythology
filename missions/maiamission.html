Mission

Maia plans to launch sometime after 2027. Its objective is to seek to understand how different types of air pollution affect human health. Maia will study the size and quantity of airborne particles to show how pollution affects pregnancy, asthma, and other conditions, both long and short-term.

Mythology

Maia was the eldest sister of the daughters of Atlas, the man cursed to hold the Earth on his shoulders. She was also the mother of Hermes. Both Atlas and Hermes are spacecrafts as well. MAIA was named after her because of her caring role as a mother, like how it takes care of the Earth and its inhabitans by caring for humanity's health. Maia was also the goddess of growth and associated with spring time.
