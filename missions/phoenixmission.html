Mission Name

Mission

Phoenix was launched in August of 2007 in order to search for evidence of past or present microbial life on Mars. It entered the Martian atmosphere in May of 2008. It was the first successful landing of a stationary soft-lander on Mars since 32 years earlier. its objective was to uncover the mysteries of the Martian arctic.

Mythology

The Phoenix is a fire bird that rises after its own destructions, rising from its ashes. The Mars Phoenix is named this because the mission was resurrected from the hardware and instruments of two previous canceled and failed missions. It was a rebirth of exisiting technology, like how the Phoenix rebirths into the beautiful, firey bird.
