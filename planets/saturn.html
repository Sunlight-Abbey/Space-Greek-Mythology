Solar System

In our solar system, Saturn is the second largest planet. It has the most spectacular rings in our solar system. Saturn's largest moon is named Titan. It is theorized that this moon could hypothetically sustain life. Saturn's rings are made up of billions of chunks of ice and rock.

Mythology

Saturn is one of the planets named after a titan as opposed to a god. Saturn is named after the King Titan Saturn, known as Cronus to the greeks. Similarly, this might be why its moon is named Titan. The ancient romans named it Saturn, acknowledging that it was the father of the planet Jupiter. Cronus was often mistaken for Chronos, who was the personification of time, and with Saturn being a god of time as well, there is a lot of confusion between distinguishing the two.
