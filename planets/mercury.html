Mercury

Greek Name Roman Name God Of:
Hermes Mercury Speed and Travel

Solar System

In our solar system, Mercury is the smallest planet and the closest to the sun. It has no moons or rings, being a solo planet. It formed 4.5 billion years ago and is the second densest planet.

Mythology

Mercury is named after the roman god, Mercury, otherwise known as Hermes to the greeks. Mercury was known as the traveler god, whenever the gods had a message to send they sent it through him because he was the quickest. The romans named Mercury after him because Mercury is the quickest planet in our solar system. It only made sense to name the fastest planet after the god of speed.
