Solar System

Mars is the fourth planet from the sun, and the only planet we have sent rovers to investigate. Mars used to be a wet, warm planet, however over time it has become cold and dry. Mars has a similar daily rotation to Earth, only taking 24.6 hours to complete a rotation.

Mythology

Mars is named after the roman god of war, Mars, otherwise known to the greeks as Ares. In ancient greece, Ares was not well liked, however the romans highly respected him, which is why they named a planet after him. They believed the red surface color of the planet reminded them of blood and fire. In mythology, Mars was a lover to Venus, despite her marriage to Vulcan.
