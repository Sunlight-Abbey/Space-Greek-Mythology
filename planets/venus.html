Solar System

In our solar system, Venus is the second planet to the sun and is the hottest. It is a neighboring planet to Earth. Venus is often called Earth's evil twin because it has a similar size and composition, but is a very hostile environment. It's crushing carbon dioxide atmosphere keeps in all the heat from the sun, practically boiling the planet.

Mythology

Venus is named after the goddess of love and beauty, Venus, also known as Aphrodite to the greeks. The ancient romans named Venus after their god of beauty because it was the brightest planet. Venus was considered to be in a loveless marriage with the god Vulcan, but her true lover was Mars. In our solar system, the lovers are split apart by Earth. Fun Fact: Julius Caesar claimed to be a descendant of Venus.
