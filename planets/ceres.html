Greek Name Roman Name God Of:
Demeter Ceres Agriculture and grain

Solar System

Ceres is the first dwarf planet that received a visit from a spacecraft. It is the largest object in an asteroid belt between Mars and Jupiter. It was at first classified as an asteroid belt, but due to its size, it got upgraded to a dwarf planet.

Mythology

Ceres was named after the goddess of agriculture, Ceres, also known to the greeks as Demeter. Ceres is mainly known for her daughter, who was kidnapped by Pluto. Due to grief and mourning, she created the seasons and put the whole world into a winter. Jupiter made a deal with Pluto to return her daughter for half of the months of the year, creating the seasons.
