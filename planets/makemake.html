Makemake

Close Equivalents Rapa Nui Name God Of:
Zeus/Jupiter, Prometheus Makemake fertility and humanity

Solar System

Makemake is a dwarf planet located in the kuiper belt. It is slightly smaller than Pluto and takes about 305 years to orbit the sun. It was originally named Easterbunny, not unlike Haumea which was originally named Santa. The surface of the planet is very cold.

Mythology

Like Haumea, Makemake is not named after a greek or roman god. Makemake is named after the supreme leader and creator of humanity of the Rapanui mythology. It does not have a direct equivalent to Greek or Roman gods, but it could be considered an equivalent to Zeus/Jupiter, the supreme leader of the gods, or Prometheus, a titan who created humanity. He appeared to be an equivalent version to Tane, a polynesian god.
