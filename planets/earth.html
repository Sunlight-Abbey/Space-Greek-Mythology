Solar System

This is our home planet! It's the third planet from the sun and the only one able to sustain life. While many think a day is 24 hours, it techncially only takes 23.9 hours to complete a rotation. Additionally, a full rotation around the sun takes 365.25 days as opposed to 365. This extra 0.25 is the reason we have a leap year every four years.

Mythology

Earth is the only planet to not be named after a roman god, at least in English. In other languages, such as Spanish, Earth is "tierra." This word comes from the latin word "Terra." In Roman mythology, Terra is the name of the goddess and personification of the Earth. She is also known as "Mother Earth." She holds close relations to the god of the Sky.
