Orion

The Constellation

Orion is a constellation representing a hunter and hero in Greek Mythology. It is one of the most recognizable constellations in the night sky, known for Orion's Belt, a line of three bright stars. It is visible in both hemispheres during winter months.

Mythology

In Greek Mythology, Orion was a great hunter known for his strength and pride. He claimed he could kill all animals on Earth, which angered the gods. A giant scorpion was sent to defeat him. After his death, he was placed in the sky as the Orion constellation.
