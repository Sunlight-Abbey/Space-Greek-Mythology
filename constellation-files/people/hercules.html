Hercules

The Constellation

Hercules is a constellation representing a legendary hero known for his strength and his twelve labors. It is located in the northern sky and is one of the largest constellations. It contains the famous Hercules Globular Cluster.

Mythology

In Greek Mythology, Hercules (Heracles) was a powerful hero known for completing twelve difficult labors to atone for his sins. Through strength and perseverance, he defeated many monsters. After his death, he was granted immortality and placed among the stars as the Hercules constellation.
