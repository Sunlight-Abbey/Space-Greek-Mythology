Perseus

The Constellation

Perseus is a constellation representing a hero known for defeating Medusa. It is located in the northern sky and is part of the same mythological story as Andromeda and Cepheus. It contains the famous variable star Algol.

Mythology

In Greek Mythology, Perseus was a hero best known for slaying Medusa and rescuing Andromeda. He used cleverness and bravery to defeat powerful enemies. After his life of heroic deeds, he was placed among the stars as the Perseus constellation.
