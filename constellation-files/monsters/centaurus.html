Centaurus

The Constellation

Centaurus is a constellation representing a centaur, a half-human half-horse creature. It is located in the southern sky and contains several bright stars, including Alpha Centauri.

Mythology

In Greek Mythology, Centaurus represents a centaur, often associated with Chiron, a wise and noble teacher. After his death, he was placed among the stars as the Centaurus constellation.
