Carina

The Constellation

Carina is a constellation representing the keel of a ship. It is part of a larger ancient constellation that represented a great ship. It is located in the southern sky.

Mythology

In Greek Mythology, Carina is part of the great ship Argo, used by Jason and the Argonauts on their quest for the Golden Fleece. It represents the keel of the ship and was placed among the stars.
