Leo

The Constellation

Leo is one of the 12 zodiac signs, and one of the three fire signs. Leo's symbol is the lion. Its dates are in between July 23 and August 22nd. Its ruling planet is the Sun. It's the fifth zodiac sign. Fun Fact: Madonna is a Leo.

Mythology

In Greek Mythology, King Eurystheus gave Heracles 12 Labors. If Heracles succeeded, he would be purified of sin, be granted immortality, and become a God. His first labor was to kill the Nemean Lion, a large lion whose hide was impervious to weapons. Heracles corned the lion in its cave and wrestled it to death by his neck. Hera placed this lion amongst the stars to honor it.
