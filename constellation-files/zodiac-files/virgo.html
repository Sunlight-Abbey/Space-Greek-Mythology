Virgo

The Constellation

Virgo is one of the 12 zodiac signs, and one of the three earth signs. Virgo's symbol is the maiden. Its dates are in between August 23 to September 22nd. Its ruling planet is Mercury. It's the sixth zodiac sign. Fun Fact: Michael Jackson is a virgo.

Mythology

In Greek Mythology, Virgo is often associated with Astraea, the maiden goddess of purity and justice. She lived among humans during the Golden Age, but as humanity became corrupt, she left Earth and ascended to the heavens. She was placed among the stars as the Virgo constellation, representing innocence and the lost age of peace.
