Libra

The Constellation

Libra is one of the 12 zodiac signs, and one of the three air signs. Libra's symbol is the weighing scales, representing balance and justice. Its dates are in between September 23 and October 22nd. Its ruling planet is Venus. It's the seventh zodiac sign. Fun Fact: Bruno Mars is a Libra.

Mythology

In Greek Mythology, Libra is associated with the scales of justice, often linked to Astraea, the goddess of fairness and innocence. She carried the scales to weigh good and evil among humanity. When the world became too corrupt, she left Earth and ascended to the heavens. Her scales were placed in the sky as the Libra constellation, symbolizing balance and justice.
