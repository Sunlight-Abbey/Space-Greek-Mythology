Taurus

The Constellation

Taurus is one of the 12 zodiac signs, and one of the three earth signs. Taurus' symbol is a bull, representing strength. Its dates are in between April 20 to May 20th. Its ruling planet is Venus. It's the second zodiac sign. Fun Fact: The Rock is a Taurus.

Mythology

In Greek Mythology, Taurus represents the great bull that Zeus transformed into to approach Europa. Disguised as a beautiful white bull, Zeus gained her trust and carried her across the sea to Crete. To commemorate this event, Zeus placed the bull among the stars as the Taurus constellation.
