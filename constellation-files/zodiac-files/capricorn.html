Capricorn

The Constellation

Capricorn is one of the 12 zodiac signs, and one of the three earth signs. Capricorn's symbol is a horned seagoat, a goat with a mermaid tail and long horns. Its dates are in between December 22 and January 19th. Its ruling planet is the Saturn. It's the tenth zodiac sign. Fun Fact: Michelle Obama is a Capricorn.

Mythology

In Greek Mythology, when the gods fled from the monster Typhoeus, Aigipan assumed the form of a fish-tailed goat. He later came to aid Zeus from the giant. In honor of his service, when Aigipan died, Zeus put him amongst the stars as the Capricorn constellation.
