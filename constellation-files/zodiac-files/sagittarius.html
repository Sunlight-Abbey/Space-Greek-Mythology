Sagittarius

The Constellation

Sagittarius is one of the 12 zodiac signs, and one of the three fire signs. Sagittarius' symbol is an arrow to represent the archer. Its dates are in between November 22 to December 21st. Its ruling planet is Jupiter. It's the ninth zodiac sign. Fun Fact: Taylor Swift is a Sagittarius.

Mythology

In Greek Mythology, Sagittarius is often associated with a centaur archer, sometimes identified as Chiron, a wise and kind teacher of heroes. Unlike other centaurs, Chiron was known for his intelligence and healing abilities. After his death, he was honored by the gods and placed in the sky as the Sagittarius constellation, aiming his arrow across the heavens.
