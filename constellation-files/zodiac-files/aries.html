Aries

The Constellation

Aries is one of the 12 zodiac signs, and one of the three fire signs. Aries' symbol represents the ram, showing off its horns. Its dates are in between March 21 and April 19th. Its ruling planet is Mars. It's the first zodiac sign. It begins the spring equinox. Fun Fact: Lady Gaga is an Aries.

Mythology

In Greek Mythology, the Aries Constellation was a Golden Ram with Golden fleece. This ram was able to fly, and was sent by a cloud nymph, Nephele, to rescue her children, who were about to be sacrificed to the Gods. The ram carried her children to the far end of the Black Sea and told the boy to sacrifice himself to the gods and lay his fleece in the grove of the god of war, Ares. Due to this sacrifice, the ram was placed amongst the Aries constellation. Later, his fleece became the goal of the hero, Jason.
