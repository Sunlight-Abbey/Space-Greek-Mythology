Gemini

The Constellation

Gemini is one of the 12 zodiac signs, and one of the three air signs. Gemini's symbol is the twins, representing dual perspective. Its dates are in between May 21 and June 20th. Its ruling planet is Mercury. It's the third zodiac sign. Fun Fact: Tom Holland is a Gemini.

Mythology

In Greek Mythology, the Dioscuri were the star-crowned twin gods of an electrical discharge which appears on the rigging of ships portending deliverance from a storm. They were gods of horsemanship and protectors of guests and travelers. They were kind and generous, so to honor them once they died, Zeus turned them into constellations.
