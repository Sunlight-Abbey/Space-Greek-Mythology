Pisces

The Constellation

Pisces is one of the 12 zodiac signs, and one of the three water signs. Pisces' symbol is two fish, swimming against each other. Its dates are in between February 19 to March 20th. Its ruling planet is Neptune. It's the twelfth and final zodiac sign. Fun Fact: Millie Bobby Brown is a Pisces.

Mythology

In Greek Mythology, Pisces represents two fish tied together by a cord. When the monster Typhon attacked the gods, Aphrodite and her son Eros transformed into fish to escape. They tied themselves together so they would not be separated in the river. To honor their escape and unity, they were placed among the stars as the Pisces constellation.
