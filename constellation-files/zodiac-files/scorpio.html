Scorpio

The Constellation

Scorpio is one of the 12 zodiac signs, and one of the three water signs. Scorpio's symbol is a scorpion, similar to its name. Its dates are in between October 23 to November 21st. Its ruling planet is Mars, sharing it with Aries, or Pluto. It's the eighth zodiac sign. Fun Fact: Katy Perry is a Scorpio.

Mythology

In Greek Mythology, Scorpio represents the giant scorpion sent by the goddess Gaia (or sometimes Artemis) to defeat Orion. Orion had grown arrogant, claiming he could kill all animals on Earth. The scorpion stung and defeated him. As a result, both were placed in the sky on opposite sides, so when Scorpio rises, Orion sets, symbolizing their eternal chase.
