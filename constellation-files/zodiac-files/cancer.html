Cancer

The Constellation

Cancer is one of the 12 zodiac signs, and one of the three water signs. Cancer's symbol represents the crab, representing its claws and balance. Its dates are in between June 21 and July 22nd. Its ruling planet is the Moon. It's the fourth zodiac sign. Fun Fact: Ariana Grande is a Cancer.

Mythology

In Greek Mythology, King Eurystheus gave Heracles 12 Labors. If Heracles succeeded, he would be purified of sin, be granted immortality, and become a God. In Heracles' second Labor, he was tasked to slay the Hydra. The Hydra was aided by a giant crab, which Heracles defeated. Hera put the Crab into the constellations in order to honor its bravery, becoming the Cancer Constellation.
