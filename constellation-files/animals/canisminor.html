Canis Minor

The Constellation

Canis Minor is a constellation representing a small dog. It is located near Canis Major and Orion. Its brightest star is Procyon, one of the brighter stars visible from Earth.

Mythology

In Greek Mythology, Canis Minor represents Orion's smaller hunting dog. Though less prominent, it remains close to its master in the sky. It symbolizes companionship and loyalty.
