Delphinus

The Constellation

Delphinus is a constellation representing a dolphin. It is located in the northern sky and is known for its distinct diamond shape. It is associated with stories of sea creatures helping gods and humans

Mythology

In Greek Mythology, Delphinus represents a dolphin who helped the god Poseidon win the love of Amphitrite. As a reward for its kindness, the dolphin was placed among the stars as the Delphinus constellation.
