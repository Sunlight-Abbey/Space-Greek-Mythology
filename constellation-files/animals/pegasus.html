Pegasus

The Constellation

Pegasus is a constellation representing a winged horse. It is located in the norhtern sky and is associated with the her Perseus. Its Great Square is a well-known asterism.

Mythology

In Greek Mythology, Pegasus was a magical winged horse born from the blood of Medusa. He helped heroes like Perseus and Bellerophon. After his service, he was placed among the stars as the Pegasus constellation.
