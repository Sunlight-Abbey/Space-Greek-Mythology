Canis Major

The Constellation

Canis Major is a constellation representing a large dog. It is located in the southern sky and follows the Orion constellation. It has Sirius, the brighest star.

Mythology

In Greek Mythology, Canis Major represents one of Orion's hunting dogs. It follows him across the sky as he hunts. The constellation contains Sirius, the brightest star, symbolizing the loyalty and strength of the dog.
