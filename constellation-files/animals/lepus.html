Lepus

The Constellation

Lepus is a constellation representing a hare. It is located just below the Orion constellation and is often depicted as being hunted by Orion and his dogs, Canis Major and Minor.

Mythology

In Greek Mythology, Lepus is a hare that sits beneath Orion's feet. It is often depicted as being hunted, symbolizing the constant chase between predator and prey.
