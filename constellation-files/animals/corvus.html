Corvus

The Constellation

Corvus is a constellation representing a crow. It is located in the southern sky and is associated with Apollo in greek mythology. It is small but easily recognizable due to its distinct shape.

Mythology

In Greek Mythology, Corvus was a crow sent by Apollo to watch over a mortal. The crow failed its task and lied, angering Apollo. As punishment, he placed the crow in the sky as the Corvus constellation.
