Ursa Major

The Constellation

Ursa Major is a constellation representing a big bear. It is one of the most famous constellations and contains the Big Dipper asterism. It is visible year-round in much of the nothern hemisphere.

Mythology

In Greek Mythology, Ursa Major represents Callisto, who was transformed into a bear by Hera. She was later placed in the sky by Zeus to protect her.
